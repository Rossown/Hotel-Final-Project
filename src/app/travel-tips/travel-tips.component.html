Travel Tips

  • Be flexible - Always plan for delays and do your best to not get upset when things go wrong, patience is important!
  • Make lists - Make lists a week or so before your trip of things you don't want to forget
  • Pack extra underwear - Better safe than sorry
  • Stay hydrated
  • Put your room number and hotel address in your phone
  • Beware of free public WiFi - The idea sounds nice but it's all too easy for hackers to steal your information on public WiFi
  • Alert your bank and credit card company of your travel plans - This way they won't put a hold on your account if they think there is suspicious activity going on
  • Wear sunscreen
  • Keep an open mind
